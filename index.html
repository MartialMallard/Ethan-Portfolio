    About Me

    ~/~/~ Don't Forget the Fun. ~/~/~

    Hi, I'm Ethan, and I'm a game designer.

    I think that one of the most important but often overlooked aspects of game design is that beyond all the technical marvel of complex game systems and eye-catching visuals, games are supposed to be fun. It's easy to lose track of that goal until you take a step back and experience it yourself.

    My job as a game designer isn't just to make sure that things work, that's a given. My goal is to provide players with an unforgettable experience.
