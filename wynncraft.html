Wynncraft

Wynncraft is an MMORPG server based in Minecraft.

My Responsibilities:

- Conceptualize, pitch, and design reimagined versions of outdated class archetypes

- Participate in group discussions involving overall ability tree balance

- Iterate through and carefully tweak balance for updated abilities and archetypes

- Collaborate with development and art teams to create brand new class abilities

- Prototype and playtest new gameplay tools surrounding the ability tree and combat/movement systems

Overview

Wynncraft is a multiplayer MMORPG server developed entirely within Minecraft. Despite the limitations brought by the server's host game, it has made many strides in making an impressive standalone play experience that separates it from its roots.

Wynncraft features 5 playable classes, each fit with 3 separate archetypes, and hundreds of unique abilities. With several major provinces to play through and hundreds of hours of content, the game offers a variety of fun and replayable experiences.

The Ability Tree

I had the opportunity to join Wynncraft's Ability Tree team where I got to work on projects in preparation for the game's upcoming endgame expansion. During my time working on the team, I was able to help lead discussion in our team to create interesting new class abilities and strategies.

My approach towards building a player's toolkit adopts a "design for use, design for abuse" philosophy. Wynncraft's ability tree shares abilities between all 3 archetypes within a class, and that ability to experiment with unique gameplay strategies allows a form of expression that I think is important for a these types of gameplay systems.

I think that a common pitfall that happens when designing in this environment is the homogenization of gameplay loops. Making sure that abilities are made to synergize with each other in intuitive ways outside of their archetype's gameplay loop not only makes things easier for newer players to understand, but also allows more experimental players to experience moments of discovery.
